Electric cars

Electric cars are an excellent way to slash your emissions and your fuel prices at the same time!

However, most people are turned off by what they see as the ridiculously high prices and lacking charging infrastructure.


However, what a lot of NZ citizens don't seem to realise is that you can actually get discounts on EVs from the government and face a tax on emission heavy cars.

Furthermore, the average price of charging your EV is around $0.30/Litre! For the average distance travelled in a car each year, it will only cost you $300 in electricity for the WHOLE YEAR.
